Hello! I am a scientist at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I am an interdisciplinary, systems researcher. Originally trained in microbiology, I now work on variety of environmental health science topics including health impacts of climate change and air quality.

I work with the Climate Solutions for Health Lab, part of Center for Sustainability of the Global Environment (SAGE) at University of Wisconsin-Madison. I am also a co-investigator on the NASA Health and Air Quality Applied Sciences Team (HAQAST).
